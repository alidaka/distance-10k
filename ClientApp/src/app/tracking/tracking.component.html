Training your plan

Pace predictors and calculators

McMillan's calculator: enter a race or pace you have done, and get expected times for other distances

Tinman: given your goal pace, figure out various workout paces (tempo, VO2Max, ...)

Workout trackers

It's important to review past workouts to understand how you're improving, monitor fatigue, and stay motivated. If nothing else, it's fun to see the maps of where you ran and share with friends.

Strava is my favorite site for this. Take a quick look at my page for an idea of what it gives you.

You can also upload to Garmin, MapMyRun, Nike+, or a variety of other trackers.

Some watches/apps automatically upload to various places. For a few reasons, I manually upload every few days.
